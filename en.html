Epidural Analgesia

Safe, effective, and reassuring pain relief for childbirth

Introduction

An epidural is a safe and effective method for managing labour pain. Once administered, it typically takes 10 to 20 minutes to take effect.

  • Decreases intensity of contraction pain
  • Reduces duration of pain during contractions
  • Allows you to rest and recover
